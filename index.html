Manual Placement

  • Click a piece, then click the board to place it.
  • Drag a piece from the tray and drop it on the board.
  • Right-click a piece to rotate it.
  • Use the arrow keys to move a selected placed piece.
  • Press Delete to remove a selected placed piece.
